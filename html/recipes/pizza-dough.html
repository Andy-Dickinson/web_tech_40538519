Pizza Dough

  • An easy dough recipe for all your pizza needs.

    Course: n/a

    Prep time: 30 minutes

    Total time: 60 minutes

Ingredients

  • 7 g Dried Yeast
  • 5 g Golden Caster Sugar
  • 20 ml Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 180 ml Lukewarm Water
  • 300 g Tipo '00' Flour or Strong White Bread Flour
  • 3 g Fine Sea Salt

Method

  1. In a jug, mix the yeast, sugar and oil into the water (1 part boiling, 2 parts cold will be perfect temperature for the yeast) and leave for 10 minutes for the yeast to rehydrate.
  2. Sive the flour and salt onto a clean work surface and make a well in the middle. Pour the yeast mix into the well.
  3. Using a fork, bring the flour in gradually from the sides and mix in. Repeat until it all starts to come together. Work the rest of the flour with your clean and flour-dusted hands.
  4. Knead for 10 minutes until springy and smooth.
  5. Place in a large flour-dusted bowl and flour the top of it. Cover the bowl with a damp cloth and place in a warm room for about an hour until double in size.
  6. Place the dough onto a flour-dusted surface and knock back (push down with fingertips to press out the air).
  7. Use immediately, or keep it wrapped in clingfilm in the fridge or freezer until required. Enjoy!
