Curry Base Sauce

  • Make authentic curries in a hurry after getting ahead by pre-preparing this great curry base.

    Freezes well and can be made in advance or in large batches.

    Course: n/a

    Prep time: 25 minutes

    Cook time: 35 minutes

    Total time: 60 minutes

Ingredients

  • 60 ml Ghee
  • 5 Large Onion(s) - Peeled & Chopped
  • 1 Bulb Garlic - Peeled
  • 10 cm Ginger - Peeled & Chopped
  • 2 Carrot(s)
  • 1 Green Pepper - Washed & Chopped
  • 1 Red Pepper - Washed & Chopped
  • 1 Can Chopped tomatoes
  • 500 ml Water
  • 15 ml Curry Powder
  • 15 ml Ground Cumin
  • 15 ml Ground Coriander
  • 15 ml Ground Turmeric
  • 15 ml Ground Feunugreek
  • 15 ml Smoked Paprika

Method

  1. Heat the oil in a large pot.
  2. Gently fry the onions for 5 minutes until softened.
  3. Add the garlic, ginger and carrots. Stir. Fry for another 5 minutes.
  4. Place the rest of the ingredients in the pot, ensuring the water covers most of the veg, add more if required.
  5. Simmer for 15-20 minutes until the veg is cooked and tender.
  6. Place the contents in a food processor and blend until smooth.
  7. Use immediately in a curry, store in the fridge for up to 4 days or portion and freeze. Enjoy!
