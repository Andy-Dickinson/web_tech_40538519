Vegetarian Margherita

  • An easy, quick and tasty meal. Fun to make together with kids or friends.

    If the dough is too wet, add a little more flour. Too stretchy, roll out and leave to rest for 10 minutes or so.

    Course: Dinner

    Plus Pizza Base: +60 minutes

    Prep time: 10 minutes

    Cook time: 10 minutes

    Total time: 20 minutes

Ingredients

  • 300 g Pizza Base Dough - see link
  • 100 ml Passata
  • 1 Handful Fresh Basil - For Sauce
  • 1 Clove Garlic - Crushed
  • 125 g Mozzarella Ball - Sliced
  • 1 Handful Parmesan - Grated
  • 1 Handful Cherry Tomatoes - Halved
  • 1 Handful Basil Leaves - Garnish

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 220C.
  2. Make the sauce: Mix the passata, basil (make sure to have some for the garnish) and crushed garlic together, then season to taste.
  3. Roll out the dough, give it a quick knead if necessary and split into a balls (one for each pizza).
  4. Place dough onto floured baking sheets.
  5. Spread sauce onto pizza base with the back of a spoon (modest is better).
  6. Sprinikle over the cheese and tomatoes and a drizzle ofo olive oil. Season to taste.
  7. Place in top shelf of oven for 8-10 minutes.
  8. Rip basil leaves and sprinkle over along with a little more olive oil.
  9. Serve and enjoy!
