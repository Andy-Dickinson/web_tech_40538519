About Us

Who We Are

Culinary Code is a recipe based website with the aim of easily finding the right recipe for you. Recipes can be found based on cuisine, course or diet. Every recipe has the option to quickly and automatically switch between imperial or metric units. As well as this, each can be adjusted for the number of portions, so you will never be left short when you have guests. Each week there is a featured recipe - always hand-picked, and ready to wow any insatiable visitors. We love our food at Culinary Code, and love to share good recipes. We strive for excellence, tasty food, easy to follow instructions along with providing a positive experience when navigating the site.

Our website has been developed for educational purposes for Web Technologies module at Edinburgh Napier University.
